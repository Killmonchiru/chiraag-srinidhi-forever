Every Moment With You

Chiraag × Srinidhi
Seeing your photos, I can tell the world was kind to you long before I came along.

But in loving you, I know it was kind to me too.

Every moment with you, every smile you carry,
is something my heart wants to hold onto forever.

I love you—not because you’re perfect,
but because you’re you.

Srinidhi, you are more than words or moments.
You are my favorite thought, my wildest dream,
and the one my heart chooses—again and again, always.
